Aliyah's Top 2 Fav Foods

Bulgogi

This is beef bulgogi, a dish made of thinly sliced beef marinated in soy sauce, garlic, sugar, and sesame oil, giving it a sweet and savory flavor.

It comes from South Korea, and its name means “fire meat” because it’s traditionally grilled. It’s often cooked right at the table and eaten with rice or wrapped in lettuce.

Personally I enjoy this with one of its most popular sides ; Bean sprouts, marinated in sesame oil.
